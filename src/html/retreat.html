Move your body. Strengthen your soul.

This April, escape to the sun-soaked island of Holbox, Mexico, for a week designed to recharge your body, calm your mind, and remind you just how strong you truly are.

Soul Strong blends movement, adventure and rest — all set against Holbox’s postcard-perfect beaches and slow-paced island rhythm. You’ll train, stretch, explore, laugh, eat incredible food, and reconnect with yourself alongside people on similar journeys of finding strength from the inside out.

Each morning, we’ll meet at the hotel for a light pre-workout snack before taking a short, scenic walk along the beach to our training spot. Morning workouts will alternate between group classes led by Adelade LaFontaine and beach bootcamps at Holbox Bootcamp.

After the workout, you can choose to join a morning yoga flow with Holbox Yoga, take a refreshing dip in the Yucatan sea, or head back to Blue Holbox for a nourishing breakfast. It’s the perfect blend of movement, nature, and ease to start your day feeling strong and grounded.

Afternoons are yours — explore the island, book a private training session with Adelade, join one of the included excursions, relax by the beach, or wander through colourful town streets

Evenings offer optional activities like Zumba, sunset kayaking, taco tours, or nights out on the town.

Why Holbox?

I chose Holbox because it’s one of those rare places that feels alive with energy yet deeply calming at the same time. I’ve travelled here multiple times, and each visit gives me that same feeling — a mix of peace, joy, and perspective you can only get from truly slowing down.

Holbox has a natural wellness to it — not because it’s curated or luxurious, but because of how effortlessly it makes you unplug. Bike barefoot through sandy streets, indulge with fresh seafood at open-air restaurants, and join the whole island on the pier to watch the evening sunsets (I’m not kidding, everyone sits and watches every night!). There’s music, laughter, incredible food, and just the right amount of nightlife if you want it — but also hammocks, quiet corners, and time to breathe.

Holbox is not a luxury all-inclusive destination — and that’s exactly why it’s perfect for Soul Strong. There are no cars here, only golf carts, ATVs, and bikes. Some streets flood after the rain, and you might see a bit of seaweed along the shore. It’s simple, sustainable, and beautifully grounded in nature.

Between the turquoise waters of the Yucatán Sea, the vibrant murals, the flamingos, and the warm local energy, Holbox is the kind of place that both grounds you and inspires you. It’s the perfect backdrop for a retreat built around movement, mindfulness, connection, and that healthy balance between challenge and chill. No strict itineraries here.

— Adelade LaFontaine, Soul Strong Creator
